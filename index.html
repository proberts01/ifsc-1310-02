    • Thoughts on Web Accessibility

    I think it is important to make websites accessable, not just because that's what everyone says, but because my grandparents have trouble viewing websites so they use the reader. There has been multiple cases where I have had to read out the options for them on general use websites that you would expect to be "up to code" but are very lackluster in options for impairments. From a SIMPLE Google search, Web accessibility is not a new concept, but it is now more relevant than ever, with almost 2 billion people living with disabilities worldwide and relying on the web for information, education, and more.
